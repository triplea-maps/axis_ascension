A WWII scenario.

Gameplay Features:
- V3 Rules.
- Collecting incomes before rounds.
- Each naval point has a 25% chance to spawn a submarine/destroyer.
- No Strategic Bombing.
- No building new factories.
- Captured factories cannot be used.
- Japan and The Soviet Union has an unbreakable non-aggression pact.
- Cairo, Panama, Gibraltar and Germany are canals.
- Combatant aaGuns.
- Harbours add +2 movements to naval units.

Countries:

Axis:
Germany
Italy
Japan

Allies:
The British Empire
The United States
The Soviet Union

Victory Condition:
Capture an enemy capital.
